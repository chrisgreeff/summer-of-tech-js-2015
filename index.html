Web Audio (flex visualisation of waveform etc)

This is something I am currently looking into, so I’ll show you a mix of my most favourite projects that I am drawing inspiration from To start this is a really cool visualisation of the data you get access to via the analyser node Created by felixturner who has actually created lots of my favourite projects Waveform – the jagged white line, it’s the shape of the sound wave as it flies through the air and hits your ear. Levels – the bar chart of frequency amplitudes, from bass on the left to treble on the right. Volume – the thicker bar on the right hand side Beat Detection – the volume bar flashes white when a beat is detected. The white line above the volume bar indicates the beat threshold. The potential from that data to create sweet visuals is crazy

Beerbot

At Vend, we have a keg and a common problem with the keg is we don’t really track when it’s finished, which has left a couple of Fridays pretty dry. And as web developers this is unacceptable So a few of us got together to hack on beerbot. A flowmeter is attached to the keg, and via an API you can receive data about how much is left, what beer is in there currently, what’s coming next, the temperature of the beer All very important facts The designs are by our very own VendHQ popmaker Jacky Lee Him, Lance, Raf and Jamie have done most of the handwork and my contribution was
